Robots

Every year, Scrappy's Garage competes in robotics competitions in the Atlanta area with our ever-growing roster of robots.

Flibbetygibbet

Flibbetygibbet is a veteran of two Robot Battles events at Dragon Con in the 30lb class. It is a 2 motor and 4 wheel build, and is powered by an electric drill battery and hobby-grade control system.

More Information

Frankenstein's Roomba

Frankenstein's Roomba is a robot that will compete at Dragon Con in 2025. It is made by the same engineers that built Tough On Babies for 2024, and has similar design goals: it uses electronic ducted fans to produce intense suction, significantly increasing its defensive capabilities as well as making much higher torque configurations viable.

More Information

The Twins

The Twins, Flip and Flop, are our cutest indistinguishable robots. Built along the same design principles, the only way to tell them apart is their characteristic colored bumpers - Flip in blue, Flop in red.
